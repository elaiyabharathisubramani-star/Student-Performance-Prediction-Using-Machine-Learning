Project Title

Student Performance Prediction Using Machine Learning

---

Problem Statement

Educational institutions often face challenges in identifying students who may perform poorly in academics. Traditional methods of evaluating student performance are time-consuming and may not provide early predictions. A Machine Learning-based Student Performance Prediction System helps analyze student data and predict academic performance, enabling timely interventions and improved educational outcomes.

---

Project Objectives

- Predict student academic performance using Machine Learning algorithms.
- Identify students at risk of poor performance.
- Analyze factors affecting student success.
- Improve decision-making for educators.
- Enhance student learning outcomes through early intervention.
- Reduce dropout rates and improve academic achievement.

Expected Outcome

The system will accurately predict student academic performance based on attendance, study habits, previous marks, and assessment scores. It will help educators identify struggling students early and provide necessary support to improve their academic success.

Project Outcome

- Accurate prediction of student performance.
- Early identification of academically weak students.
- Improved educational planning and student guidance.
- Better decision-making for teachers and administrators.
- Enhanced student success rates and reduced failure rates.

The Student Performance Prediction Using Machine Learning system provides an intelligent approach to analyzing student data and forecasting academic outcomes.
